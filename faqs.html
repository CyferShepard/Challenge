Frequently Asked Questions

Workshop

  • How long does a service take?

  • We strive to achieve a 10 working day cycle on all machines unless exceptional repair work is needed.

  • Are machines tested before leaving the workshop?

  • All machines are comprehensively “stitched out” to verify that the machine is performing to its specification.

  • What is the warrantee on a service?

  • Workmanship and parts are warranted for 3 months.

  • What must I bring in when I book my machine for a service?

  • When You Bring Your Machine in for Servicing, please bring along the following:
    • Sewing Machine
    • Embroidery Unit (If Applicable)
    • Power Cord & Foot Control
    • All Purpose Presser Foot
    • Embroidery Presser Foot (Embroidery Units Only)
    • Bobbin Case
    • Bobbin
    • General Sewing Stitch Plate (Not Straight Stitch Plate)
    Please do not bring any other accessories. If you are experiencing trouble with a specific problem, please bring in the sample of what the machine is doing.

  • How often should I service my machine?

  • To keep your machine running great, you should service it at least once a year – If you sew more than 4 hours or so every day, you should service more frequently.

  • Why use a Bernina Authorised Service Agent?

  • If you get your Bernina or Bernette Sewing Machine or Overlocker serviced by a non Authorised Mechanic you void your Bernina and Bernette warranty.

  • Do you only service Bernina machines?

  • We service all makes of sewing machines and overlockers (Terms and Conditions apply).
